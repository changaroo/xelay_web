XELAY CAPITAL

Xelay Capital is the advisory investment affiliate of Xelay Acumen. The principal investment objective of the Fund is to achieve capital appreciation through investing in primarily in publicly traded equity securities and exchange traded funds. Xelay Capital leverages an experienced team of quantitative oriented investment professionals to achieve its investment objectives.

RESULTS

The Xelay Capital team evaluates investments based on in-depth quantitative and financial analysis. The Xelay Capital Fund has outperformed the major market indexes in 7 out of 8 years and on average returns 28% per year since its inception in late 2007.
